Spotlight

Contento

Rating: 4.5

Category: Peruvian Wine Bars

Address: 88 E 111th St, New York, NY 10029

Description: Contento is a restaurant built with accessibility in mind. There is a small incline from the street to the door. There is variety in seating arrangements for different needs. There is low section in the bar. The bathroom has hand bars. There is wide space in between the tables.

About

It all started with two foodies who are passionate about accessibility. One is a wheelchair user and the other is a coder. My friend, Selise and I, love to explore different restaurants aroud the city! We always call ahead of time to inquire about the accessibility of the restaurant. However, accessibility means different things to different people. Sometimes the calls are a hit! But other times, we faceplant on the dirty concrete streets due to ramps of wrong dimension... I made this website to compile a list of accessible restaurants near Columbia Universtiy along with description of various features, so you can get the information you need without doing the phone call and hopefully avoid tasting the streets of New York!
